Jørn Lande (born May 30, 1970), is a Norwegian hard rock and heavy metal vocalist renowned for his exceptional vocal range and power. He has achieved widespread recognition as a solo artist and for his collaborations with numerous bands, including Masterplan, Ark, and Beyond Twilight. Lande's impressive vocal prowess, spanning from soaring highs to deep growls, has earned him comparisons to legendary singers like Ronnie James Dio. With a discography spanning several decades, Jørn Lande continues to be a prominent figure in the world of heavy metal, captivating audiences with his dynamic performances and enduring musical talent.
